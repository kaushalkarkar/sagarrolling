What We Offer

Our Services

We manufacture and supply all types of rolling shutters for residential, commercial, and industrial use — built to last.

🏭

Manual Rolling Shutters

Heavy-duty galvanized steel rolling shutters for shops, warehouses, and factories. Smooth operation with durable locking systems.

⚙️

Gear Type Shutters

Spring-balanced gear type rolling shutters with easy hand-operated mechanism. Ideal for medium to large openings.

⚡

Electric & Remote Shutters

Motorized rolling shutters with remote control for ultimate convenience. Smooth, quiet, and fully automated operation.

Rolling Grill Shutters

Ventilated rolling grills for showrooms, banks, and retail outlets. Provides security while maintaining visibility and airflow.

🔧

Fabrication Works

Custom steel fabrication, repairs, and AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts) for all types of shutters and gates.

📦

Raw Material Supply

Rolling shutter lath, bottom rail, side guides, springs, locks and all steel raw materials supplied at wholesale rates.
